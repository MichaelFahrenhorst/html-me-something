My Great Journey to Cancun!

A litte bit about Mexico!

  • The official name of for Mexico is the United Mexican States.
  • Mexico is the 14th largest country by land area.
  • There are 31 states in Mexico as well as the capital city (Mexico City).
  • The highest mountain in Mexico is Pico de Orizaba, a dormant volcano that reaches 5,636 metres (18,491 ft) above sea level.
  • Mexico hosted the Football World Cup in both 1970 and 1986.

Snorkeling

Going snorkeling is a very cool under water adventure! I went while in Mexico right by the resort I was staying at.
For those that have never done it, a snorkel is an under water breathing aparatus. You're able to stay underwater for
extended amount of time and see the beautiful nature below the water. One of my favorite parts was being able to swim by
different types of fish below in the deep blue sea!

Scuba Diving

I would have to say out of all the neat things I did in Mexico, scuba diving was the best! I was able to sail out 8 miles off the
coast of mexico and deep water dive. Being able to be down that deep with all the different assortments of fish was a great thing
to check off of my personal bucket list.

Laying on the Beach

All together my vacation was very enjoyable. The most relaxing thing you can do on vacation, Is lay on the beach and hang out!
Within the first day I had already burnt my skin to a crisp, but it was all worth it. I played sand volleyball, built sand-castles, and
ran through the tide from the ocean. Needless to say, I came back to St. Louis like a lobster due to the extreme burn from the sun.
If I could do it all over again I wouldn't change a thing.
